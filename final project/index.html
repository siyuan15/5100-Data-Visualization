Uber pickup in New York City

Data Visualization - Project 1

From July to Septembr 2014, The average number of daily pickups of Uber
in New York City was 28842, approximately 10 times more than Lyft(2909).
Graphs below shows how the amount of Uber pickup changes corresponding
to pickup location, demographic informations, and time.

This is the number of Uber pickups per day in New York City from July 1 to September 30, 2014.
Maximum number of pickups for Uber happened on 09/13/2015 with 43205
while for Lyft it happended on 09/08/2014 with 7740 pickups.

Time VS. Number of Pickup

This heat map shows the average number of Uber pickups per hour from Monday to Sunday.
Circle are sized and colored based number of pickups in that time frame.
People tend to request Uber more towards midnight on Sunday and Monday, and more
towards 6pm from Tuesday to Saturday. Peak number of pickup(2423) happens from 5pm to 6pm
on Friday and minimun number of pickup(120) from 2am to 3am on Wednesday.
